Tikka Masala

Home

Description

Tikka Masala is a popular indian-inspired dish consisting of bits of chicken in a creamy and spicy sauce.

Ingredients

Chicken prep:

  • 4 pieces of chicken breast, sliced into big chunks
  • 3 tbs of cooking oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, shredded
  • 2 tsp of garam masala
  • 2 tsp of turmeric
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of chili powder

Sauce:

  • 1 big piece of yellow onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 piece of green chili pepper, sliced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, shredded or chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, roughly chopped
  • 1 tbs ginger, shredded
  • 1 tbs of tomato paste
  • 1 tsp of turmeric
  • 1 tbs of garam masala
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, Ã  400 g
  • 3 dl of water
  • 2 dl of heavy cream
  • 1 tbs of salt
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 tsp of black pepper

Serve with:

  • rice
  • cilantro
  • naan

Steps

  1. Dice the chicken in rough pieeces. Season with spices, garlic and salt. Roast in oven at 200 degrees (Celsius) for about 15-20 min. Let it rest.
  2. Fry the onion, chili pepper, garlic and ginger at medium heat for a few minutes.
  3. Stir the tomato paste on top and fry for 1 min.
  4. Add spices, tomatoes, water, salt, sugar and black pepper. Boil for at least 10 min on medium heat.
  5. Pour the cream.
  6. Using a hand mixer, mix the ingredients and boil for an additional 10 min.
  7. Chop the bell pepper in rough pieces and add to the pot. Add the chicken.
  8. Serve with rice and cilantro!

Risotto

Home

Description

Risotto is an Italian rice dish cooked with broth and wine until the rice reaches a creamy consistency.

Ingredients

  • 3 shallots
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 9 dl of mushroom broth
  • Olive oil
  • 3 dl of carnaroli or arborio
  • 2 dl of white wine
  • 250 g of mushroom (portobello or chanterelle)
  • 3 tbs of butter
  • 2 dl of parmesan, shredded
  • salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and finely chop the shallots and garlic.
  2. Boil the broth and keep it warm.
  3. Lightly fry the shallots and garlic in olive oil.
  4. Add the rice fry until gleaming.
  5. Add the wine and let it boil.
  6. Add 3-4 dl of broth to the rice and simmer whilst stirring.
  7. Add 1 dl of broth at a time as the rice absorbs the broth.
  8. The risotto is ready after about 15-20 min. Taste a few corn rice after consistency.
  9. Fry the mushroom in butter and season with salt and pepper.
  10. Add the mushroom to the rice.
  11. Add 2 dl of parmesan.
  12. the risotto should be creamy. Add more broth and simmer as necessary.
  13. Season with salt and pepper.
  14. Serve on pre-heated plates!

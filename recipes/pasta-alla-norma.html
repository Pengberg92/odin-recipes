Description

Pasta alla Norma is a delicious Sicilian pasta dish consisting of eggplant, marinara, basil, and parmesan.

Ingredients

Marinara:

  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, Ã  400 g
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 0,5 tsp of black pepper
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 dl of water

Miscellaneous:

  • Olive oil
  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 10-20 mini tomatoes, sliced in half
  • 0,5 tsp chili flakes
  • salt
  • fresh basil
  • parmesan (pecorino will also do)

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients of the marinara and let it simmer for about 10-20 min.
  2. Fry the eggplant in olive oil on medium heat for about 5-7 min.
  3. Add the garlic, chili flakes, and fry for an additional 1-2 min.
  4. Turn of the stove and add the halved tomatoes.
  5. Add the marinara. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Boil the pasta and mix it with the marinara sauce.
  7. Serve with shredded parmesan and fresh basil!
